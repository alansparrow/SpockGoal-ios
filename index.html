Spock Goal

Turn your effort into insightful NUMBER

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

Welcome to Spock Goal Pages.

This is an app to record how much time you have invested in building your skills. The basic idea of this app is based on the book Outliers - to be a real expert in any field, you should invest at least 10,000 hours of training.
